1. Run the .JAR file.
2. Enter the player names in the four upper fields.
3. Enter the number of days since they last visited a museum (used for calculating who plays first; the second, third and fourth player are cycled from there regardless of their number).
4. Press the Submit button.
5. Press the Start Game button to begin.
6. Play until there are 16 Landslide tiles in the Entry Area as a consequence of drawing.
7. At that point the game GUI will close and a dialogue window will appear, showing the points of each player.
8. Press Exit on that to finish.
